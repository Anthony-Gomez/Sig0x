# Sig0x

Sig0x is an AI security agent designed to protect multisig transactions from modern threats. It integrates with your existing multisig wallet and analyzes every transaction before you sign, detecting attacks such as address poisoning, blind signing, social engineering, protocol exploits, and more.

## Key Features
- **Real-time AI analysis**: Detects 12 threat vectors before every signature.
- **Address poisoning detection**: Identifies suspicious addresses and malicious clusters.
- **Calldata decoding**: Makes every function call human-readable and understandable.
- **Policy verification**: Applies your security rules, spending limits, and whitelists.
- **Signed security report**: Provides a detailed verdict to each signer (green light or red flag).
- **Multisig compatibility**: Works with Safe{Wallet}, BitGo, Casa, Unchained Capital, Nunchuk, TotalSig, Electrum, Armory, and more.

## Why Sig0x?
- $3B+ lost to multisig exploits.
- Attacks target the signing process, not the cryptography.
- Sig0x acts as an autonomous reviewer, reducing human and technical risks.

## How it works
1. **Propose a transaction** in your multisig wallet.
2. **Sig0x intercepts and analyzes** the transaction automatically (recipient identity, history, policy, risks, etc.).
3. **Receive a clear report** and recommendation before signing.

## Covered Scenarios
- Address poisoning
- Blind signing (unreadable calldata)
- Social engineering and signer collusion
- Policy drift and silent exceptions
- Cross-chain replays
